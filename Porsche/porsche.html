About Porsche

Porsche, founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche in Stuttgart, Germany, originally served as a vehicle design and engineering consultancy. Its first project was developing the Volkswagen Beetle, one of the most iconic cars in history. In 1948, Porsche produced its first car, the 356, a lightweight, rear-engine sports car that established the brand's reputation for performance and innovation. The company gained global acclaim with the launch of the Porsche 911 in 1964, a model that became a symbol of engineering excellence and timeless design. Today, Porsche continues to blend luxury, cutting-edge technology, and motorsport heritage in its vehicles, solidifying its place as one of the world's most prestigious automotive brands.
