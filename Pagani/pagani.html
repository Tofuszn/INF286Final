About Pagani

Pagani Automobili, founded in 1992 by Horacio Pagani in Modena, Italy, is a boutique hypercar manufacturer celebrated for blending artistic design with cutting-edge engineering. Horacio, originally from Argentina, honed his skills working with Lamborghini before pursuing his dream of creating his own cars. Pagani's debut model, the Zonda, launched in 1999, stunned the automotive world with its distinctive styling, meticulous craftsmanship, and use of advanced materials like carbon fiber. The company continued its legacy with the Huayra, a hypercar that pushed boundaries in aerodynamics and luxury. Today, Pagani represents the epitome of bespoke automotive artistry, producing highly exclusive vehicles for passionate collectors worldwide.
