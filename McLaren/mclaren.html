About McLaren

McLaren Automotive, part of the British McLaren Group, is a premier manufacturer of high-performance luxury sports and supercars. The companyâ€™s roots trace back to 1963 when Bruce McLaren, a New Zealand-born race car driver, founded McLaren Racing. Initially focusing on Formula 1, the team became a dominant force, winning numerous championships. McLaren transitioned into road cars with the revolutionary McLaren F1 in 1992, a model famed for its innovative design and record-setting speed. In 2010, McLaren Automotive launched as a dedicated car manufacturer, introducing models like the MP4-12C, P1, and the modern 720S. Known for their lightweight engineering, cutting-edge aerodynamics, and Formula 1-inspired technology, McLaren continues to push boundaries in both motorsport and road car performance.
